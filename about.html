Our independent café is named for the library’s most generous supporter, Audrey Geisel, aka “Mrs. Seuss.” We strive to embody the values she represented as a philanthropist to give back to the community and to support student learning.




We embrace sustainability practices and processes at all levels. Audrey’s uses local, organic, fair trade coffee beans from Cafe Virtuoso and eco-friendly packaging.




As a student-run business, we currently employ over 70 students. All workers will go through a training program so barista experience, while desirable, is not necessary.
